GamePlay
This is a game of almost checkers. The men can move forwards and back as if they were "kings".
  1. To move, simply click a tile to "pick it up" and click a second time where you would like to put it down.
  2. If a men are jumped during a turn, click "Pick Up a Piece" and then click on all men to be removed.
  3. Once all approprate men have been removed, click the "Done clearing pieces." button, and then the "End Player Turn" to complete your turn.
  4. The game will end upon turn change once either player has 4 or fewer tiles remaining.
  5. Once either player has won, click "Restart Game" if you wish to play again.
